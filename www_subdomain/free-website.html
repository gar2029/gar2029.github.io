The Steps of Building a Free Website

The World Wide Web is growing. There are millions upon millions of websites out there. No matter what you're looking for or what question you have, it's likely that you'll be able to find a website that addresses it. But is your website out there?

There are a lot of great reasons to create a free website. Perhaps you want a free way to keep in touch with family and friends or maybe you need an outlet to share your unique hobby or interest with the world. Maybe you want to create a site to share information about an event you're planning or you want to tell everyone about your favorite band. Perhaps you even want free online promotion for your business. The possibilities are endless.

Fortunately, creating your own website can be easy! Are you ready to get started creating your own free website? Here are some of the most important steps you'll be following in the process.

Planning and Preparation

If you haven't done it yet, you'll want to get your free website started by choosing a topic for it. Along with your topic, think about your goals for the site. Who do you want to see and interact with it? What type of content will you be adding to your site as you go along? Knowing the answers to these and similar questions will help you make decisions as you go through the process of creating your site.

Selecting a Web Hosting Company

Choosing a web host is a critical step of creating your free website. Without web hosting, your site can't be accessed by others through the web. Freeservers offers a variety of web hosting packages, including reliable free web hosting, to give you the options you need to create the website you want.

Registering a Domain Name

The other absolutely necessity for a website, in addition to web hosting, is a domain name. A domain name allows people to find and access your site. You must register the domain name that you choose, which makes it your domain name and establishes where it can be found. Freeservers can help you register your domain name quickly and easily.

Creating Your Web Pages

Now comes the time to fire up your creativity! There are many ways to design and create web pages. You may want to write your pages in HTML or you may want to use the easy SiteBuilder Utility that Freeservers provides to lay out your web pages. No matter which method you choose, this is the step in which you add your own text, photos, and any other content to your website.

Maintaining and Expanding Your Site

Once you've gotten to this point, you've got plenty of opportunities to continue developing and expanding your free website. Freeservers can help you add even more functionality and exciting content to your site with features like a blog, forum, calendar, and more.
