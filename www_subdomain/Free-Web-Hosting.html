What is web hosting?

A website is essentially a collection of files that is shared with others on the Internet. Just about anyone can create a website and establish an online presence. Building your own website means not only selecting the files you want to post and designing how your site will look; it also means you will need to find a way to make your site accessible to other people. A web host provides space on its server to store your pages and allow other computers around the world to access your site by means of a network or modem. It gives you disk space for your files, bandwidth for other users to access your content, and possibly other technology tools to help you build and maintain your site. No matter what kind of website you create and whether it's for business or personal use, you will need this service. Choosing a host is a critical part of creating any website.

How does free web hosting work?

Free web hosting is, just like it sounds, a service that stores and delivers your pages for free. With all the critical services that a web host provides, how can a company do it without charging the user? Generally, free web hosts support free sites with advertisements. That means that when visitors access your website, they will see sponsor ads, in the form of banners or pop-ups, in addition to your content.

Is free right for me?

The answer to this question depends upon the goals you have in mind for your site. Using free web hosting is an economical option if you are looking to establish a beginning web presence with a personal website. If your site is intended primarily for viewing by family and friends, especially, having an ad-supported site will not pose a problem. However, if you intend your website to be a way to promote your business, you may want to consider upgrading to paid hosting services. When you choose a paid hosting package, your site will have a more professional appearance. Additionally, paid hosting generally offers a greater selection of features that you can use to build, maintain, and expand your website far into the future.

How do I select a hosting company?

Because the services a web host provides are so critical to the performance of your website, it's important to select the company that is right for you. If you opt for free hosting services, it can be difficult to differentiate between all the web hosts out there offering services for free at first glance.
