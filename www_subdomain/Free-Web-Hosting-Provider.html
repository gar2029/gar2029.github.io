Is a Free Web Hosting Provider Too Good to Be True?

You've undoubtedly heard the phrase, "there's no such thing as a free lunch," and this truism may have you wondering whether free web hosting is too good to be true. The reality is that free web hosting can be a valid offer and a great deal, but you do need to make sure that you're working with the right free web hosting provider and that free hosting is a good match for your needs. Here are some of the considerations you should investigate before you sign up for a free web hosting package.

How Do They Do It?

When a company offers a free service to its customers, it only makes sense for those customers to ask how the free service makes financial sense for the company. In the case of free web hosting, Freeservers is able to offer this free service by supporting its free websites with advertising. Having banner and pop-up ads on your website is a tradeoff, but for many users, the benefit of getting hosting for free is worth it.

Picking the Right Free Web Host

There are a lot of web hosting providers out there, and not all of them are created equal. Some web hosts do not provide the service and support that their customers really need. When you're choosing a host, it's important to investigate what you'll receive from the company. Here are a few points to learn about before you choose a web host.

Look for a Good Track Record

In a field filled with plenty of companies providing web hosting services, it stands to reason that not all of them are going to last long. A fly-by-night free web hosting provider that goes out of business can leave your site high and dry. You need to choose a company with a proven track record to ensure that your site will not lose its host and disappear from the internet. Freeservers has been providing web hosting services and supporting millions of websites for more than 10 years, so you can rest assured that we'll be here to provide hosting services for your website.

Newer hosting companies may lack the resources and experience that are vital to providing reliable and stable web services. While "getting in on the ground floor" can be exciting in some situations, signing up with a beginner web host usually isn't one of them. Freeservers has years of successful experience to demonstrate that we provide the expertise and services that website owners really need.

Getting Support When You Need It

When you need help with your website, you'll want to have customer support available from your hosting company. Before you sign up for a free hosting plan, make sure that your host offers the support you'll need. Freeservers offers a robust set of FAQs to assist you quickly with many of the issues you may encounter with your free website. Think about Features

You might not yet be sure exactly what your free website will be like, but it's still an important thing to think about before you sign up with a host. You'll want to have some idea of how much webspace and bandwidth you'll need as well as which website building features are most important to you. Having even a vague estimate about these matters will help you to pick the right free web host as well as the right hosting package.

After your website - and your expertise - grows, you may find that you want to add more hosting resources or features to your website. As you research free hosting packages, make sure that the free web host you choose has the flexibility and expertise to offer you the additional options you may want in the future. Preparation Pays Off

Free web hosting can make starting your own website easy and financially painless, but it can also have some pitfalls.
