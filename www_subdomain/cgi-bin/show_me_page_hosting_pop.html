Email
Email is a vital part of today's world. Whether you use email to stay in touch with family and friends or you use it to run your business, it's important to have an email package that provides you with all the features you deserve. All hosting accounts include a powerful email and calendaring feature. All accounts receive:
  • Multiple personal email addresses - yourname@yourdomain.com
  • Large Inbox (2-10GB) and large attachment size (25MB)
  • Shared Calendars
  • Spam/Virus Protection

Premium members receive additional benefits, such as:

  • File storage
  • Shared contacts/files
  • Instant Messaging
Email Spam/Virus Protection
Protection for your e-mail account against unwanted, unsolicited e-mails (SPAM) and malicious attacks from viruses that may be hidden in innocent looking emails.
